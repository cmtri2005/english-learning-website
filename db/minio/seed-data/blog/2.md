# Master Professional Meeting Vocabulary

> **Goal:** Sound confident and professional in meetings by using clear, natural phrases.

## Opening a Meeting

Use these to start smoothly and set expectations:

- **“Let’s get started.”** — Begin the meeting  
- **“The purpose of today’s meeting is…”** — State the objective  
- **“Does everyone have the agenda?”** — Ensure preparation  

**Pro tip:** After stating the purpose, add a time check:  
> “We have 30 minutes today, so let’s stay focused.”



## Expressing Opinions

Use these to share ideas politely and clearly:

- **“In my opinion…”** — Share your view  
- **“I’d like to point out that…”** — Highlight something important  
- **“From my perspective…”** — Offer your viewpoint  

**Useful follow-up:**  
> “Does anyone see it differently?”



## Making Suggestions

Use these to propose solutions and move things forward:

- **“Why don’t we…”** — Propose an idea  
- **“I suggest that we…”** — Make a recommendation  
- **“How about…”** — Offer an alternative  

**Optional add-on for clarity:**  
> “To be specific, we could…”
