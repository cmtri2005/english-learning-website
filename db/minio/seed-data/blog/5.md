
# Transform Your English in 30 Days

> **Consistency wins.** This 30-day challenge gives you a clear daily plan to improve vocabulary, grammar, listening, speaking, reading, and writing.

## Week 1: Foundation Building (Days 1–7)

**Goal:** Strengthen core vocabulary + refresh grammar basics.

**Daily time:** **30 minutes**

- **10 minutes:** Learn **10 new words**  
- **10 minutes:** Grammar exercises  
- **10 minutes:** Listening practice  

✅ **End-of-week check (Day 7):**  
- Review all 70 words  
- Write 5 sentences using new vocabulary  



## Week 2: Speaking Practice (Days 8–14)

**Goal:** Build speaking confidence and improve pronunciation.

**Daily plan:**
- Daily conversation practice (**15 minutes**)  
- Shadowing exercises (**10 minutes**)  
- Recording yourself (**5 minutes**)  

✅ **End-of-week check (Day 14):**  
- Record a **1–2 minute** talk about your week  
- Re-listen and note 3 things to improve (stress, speed, clarity)



## Week 3: Reading and Writing (Days 15–21)

**Goal:** Improve comprehension and written accuracy.

**Suggested routine (30–40 minutes/day):**
- Read 1 short article or story (15–20 minutes)  
- Highlight 5 useful phrases (5 minutes)  
- Write a short paragraph summary (10–15 minutes)

✅ **End-of-week check (Day 21):**  
- Write a **150–200 word** opinion paragraph on a simple topic.



## Week 4: Integration (Days 22–30)

**Goal:** Combine all skills with real-world practice.

**Suggested routine (30–45 minutes/day):**
- Watch/listen to real content (10–15 minutes)  
- Speak: summarize out loud (10 minutes)  
- Write: short reflection/journal (10–15 minutes)  
- Review vocabulary (5 minutes)

✅ **Final challenge (Day 30):**  
- Record a **2–3 minute** talk + write a **200-word** summary on the same topic.



## Tips to Stay Consistent

- Keep tasks small and repeatable  
- Track your streak (calendar or checklist)  
- Focus on progress, not perfection
